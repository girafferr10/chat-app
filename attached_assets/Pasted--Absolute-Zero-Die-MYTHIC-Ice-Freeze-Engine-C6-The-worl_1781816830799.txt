# Absolute Zero Die

MYTHIC

Ice

Freeze Engine

C6

"The world does not move. It waits."

When Absolute Zero arrives, time itself forgets how to flow.

HP
1320

ATK
148

DEF
92

SPD
98

Basic

Cold Truth

Deal Ice damage.

Apply 1 Chill Stack.

Skill

Deep Freeze

Apply 5 Chill to all enemies.

Enemies at 10 Chill become Frozen:
• Skip next action
• Take 25% increased damage

Ultimate

Heat Death

All enemies instantly gain 8 Chill.

For 2 turns:

• Chill cannot decay

• Frozen enemies take double Break damage

Passive

Frozen State

Enemies lose 1 SPD per Chill Stack.

Minimum SPD cannot go below 20.

Constellations

C1
Start battle with 3 Chill on all enemies.

C2
+15% DEF.

C3
Deep Freeze applies 7 Chill instead.

C4
+10 SPD.

C5
Frozen enemies take +10% extra damage from all sources.

C6
Frozen enemies have a 20% chance to skip an additional turn after thawing.
