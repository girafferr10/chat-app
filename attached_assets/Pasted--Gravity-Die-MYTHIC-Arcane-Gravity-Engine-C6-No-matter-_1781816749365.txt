# Gravity Die

Arcane

Gravity Engine

C6

"No matter how high they rise... everything falls."

Entire battlefields have collapsed beneath a single roll.

1360

142

DEF
90

SPD
96

Basic

Crush

Deal Arcane damage.

Apply one Gravity Stack.

Skill

Increase every enemy's Gravity Stack by 3.

Enemies with 5 Gravity become Weighed Down:
• -20 SPD
• Take 15% more damage.

Ultimate

Singularity

Create a Black Hole for 3 turns.

At the end of every turn:

• Pull all enemies together.

• Deal Arcane damage.

• Increase Gravity by 2.

Enemies lose 2 SPD for every Gravity Stack.

Maximum 20 SPD reduction.

C1

C2
+15% DEF.

C3

C4
+10 SPD.

C5
Black Hole lasts 1 additional turn.

C6
Enemies reaching maximum Gravity immediately lose their next turn.
